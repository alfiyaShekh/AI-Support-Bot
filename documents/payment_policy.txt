Payment Policy

We accept UPI, Credit Cards, Debit Cards, Net Banking, and selected digital wallets. All payments are processed through secure and encrypted payment gateways.

Customers should verify payment details before completing a transaction. Payment confirmation is sent through email and SMS immediately after successful payment.

In case of payment failure, customers may retry using the same or a different payment method. Failed transactions are usually reversed automatically by the bank within 3 business days.

If an amount is deducted but the order is not confirmed, customers should contact support and provide transaction details. Refunds for duplicate or incorrect charges are processed after verification.
