Cancellation Policy

Customers may cancel an order before it has been shipped. Orders can be cancelled through the My Orders section of the website.

Once an order has been shipped, cancellation is no longer possible. Customers may instead use the return process after receiving the product.

Refunds for cancelled orders are initiated within 5 business days and are credited to the original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on the bank or payment provider.

For special situations such as incorrect orders, duplicate purchases, or technical issues, customers should contact customer support for assistance.
