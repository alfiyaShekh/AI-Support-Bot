Shipping Policy

Orders are processed within 24 hours of successful payment. Customers receive an order confirmation email and SMS after placing an order.

Standard delivery usually takes between 3 and 7 business days depending on the delivery location. International orders may take up to 15 business days.

Customers can track their orders through the My Orders section on the website. Tracking information becomes available once the order has been shipped.

Shipping delays may occur during public holidays, festivals, severe weather conditions, or other unforeseen circumstances. Customers will be notified if significant delays are expected.

If an order is marked as delivered but has not been received, customers should contact support within 48 hours for investigation.
